Our Transformer Testing Solutions

Discover a comprehensive suite of solutions to enhance testing protocols, delivering peak performance and regulatory compliance.

AI Powered Advanced Fully Automatic Transformer Test bench

Experience the world’s most advanced transformer testing technology with Electrosoft’s AI-powered system. Featuring voice control, a large 43-inch interactive display, and optional automation modules, this fully automatic test bench delivers unmatched speed, precision, and reliability. Built to meet global standards and backed by full-service support, it sets a new benchmark in transformer testing.

Fully Automatic Transformer Test Bench

Electrosoft’s Fully Automatic Transformer Testing System delivers unmatched reliability, automation, and precision. Powered by the robust X’mer Edge software, it performs all critical transformer tests with minimal connection changes, ensuring fast, repeatable, and audit-ready results. Built to IEC and IEEE standards and equipped with optional automation features, it’s the ideal solution for high-performance transformer testing.

Semi-automatic Transformer Test Bench

Electrosoft’s Semi-Automatic Transformer Testing System offers the perfect balance of control, efficiency, and reliability. Designed with a robust PLC and HMI-based interface, it enables smooth, accurate execution of all critical transformer tests with just three simple connections. Ideal for manufacturers seeking dependable performance with room to scale.

Conventional Transformer Test Bench

Electrosoft’s Conventional Transformer Testing System delivers dependable, cost-effective testing with complete manual control. Featuring a rugged design and joystick-controlled excitation, it’s ideal for low- to medium-volume manufacturers and R&D labs seeking reliable performance without compromising precision or standards compliance.

LV Test trolley Refer

Electrosoft’s LV Test Trolley is a compact, mobile solution designed to streamline low-voltage transformer testing. Built for durability and seamless integration with any of our test systems, it brings unmatched flexibility, reduces setup time, and improves efficiency across your testing floor.
